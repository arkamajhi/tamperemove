The Deposit-Safe Cleaning Guarantee

TOAS inspections are rigorous. Even in a well-maintained Paawola apartment, simple cleaning oversights can result in deposit deductions. Our team cleans to the exacting standards of the official TOAS checklist:

Kitchen Hygiene Mastery

We perform deep degreasing of the range hood and thoroughly clean behind and under kitchen appliances—the most frequent failure point in student housing inspections.

Wet Room & Drain Sanitization

We tackle the floor drain (lattiakaivo), scrub all shower tiles, and descale faucets to restore bathroom hygiene to inspection-ready status.

Airflow & Ventilation

Dust buildup in air intake and exhaust vents is often missed by residents. We ensure every vent is clear to meet housing maintenance requirements.

Final Detail Finishing

From cleaning the window tracks where dust settles to scrubbing baseboards and switches, we leave your apartment spotless for the next tenant.
