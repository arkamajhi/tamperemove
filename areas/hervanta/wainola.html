The Deposit-Safe Cleaning Guarantee

The TOAS move-out inspection is a standard process, but the details matter. Our cleaning team focuses on the specific high-failure areas identified by TOAS inspectors at Wäinolä:

Kitchen & Appliance Deep-Clean

Degreasing the range hood, scrubbing behind the stove/fridge, and cleaning the interior of cabinets—the most common areas flagged in TOAS walkthroughs.

Wet Room & Drain Hygiene

Full scrub of the floor drain (lattiakaivo), scale removal from fixtures, and deep cleaning of bathroom tiles and shower glass.

Ventilation & Airflow

We ensure all air vents are wiped clean and cleared of dust to maintain proper building ventilation, as required by housing standards.

Final Detail Work

Detailed cleaning of window tracks, door frames, and light switches, leaving the unit spotless and inspection-ready.
