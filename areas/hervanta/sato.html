SATO Apartment Moving & Cleaning

Moving out of a SATO apartment requires more than just boxes and a van. SATO’s move-out inspection process is highly professional, and they require the apartment to be returned to a specific "move-in ready" state. We specialize in SATO-compliant logistics and deep-cleaning.

The SATO Inspection Standard

Unlike dormitory-style housing, SATO properties are inspected with a focus on long-term maintenance and professional hygiene. Our team ensures you meet every point on the SATO cleaning guide:

  • Kitchen: Deep-cleaning of all appliances, including the oven, dishwasher (filter and interior), and the extractor hood/grease filter.
  • Bathroom: Descaling of shower fixtures, thorough scrubbing of floor drains (lattiakaivo), and wall tile sanitation.
  • Windows & Surfaces: Cleaning both sides of all windows and wiping down all internal doors, baseboards, and light switches.
  • General: Complete vacuuming and mopping of all floors, including unreachable areas behind movable furniture.
