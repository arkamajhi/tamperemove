Moving at Mikontalo: The Full Guide

Insinöörinkatu 60, 33720 Tampere — Everything you need to know.

Mikontalo is the legendary heart of Tampere student life. Moving here involves specific challenges: massive building volume, high-demand elevator usage, and strict TOAS deposit-return criteria. Whether you are moving into your first studio or moving out to start your career, this guide covers how to execute a perfect move at Insinöörinkatu 60.

Phase 1: Logistical Planning (The "Pre-Move")

Don't wait until the last minute. Mikontalo's size means that logistical preparation is 80% of the battle.

  • Elevator Reservation: Always book your elevator slot via the TOAS portal well in advance, especially during the "Semester Change" window (end of July/August).
  • Parking Access: Our team uses the designated loading zones. We advise residents to clear the area in front of the entrance 30 minutes before arrival if possible.
  • Floor Access: We have mapped out the quickest routes from the loading dock to both the high-rise and low-rise sections of the complex.

Phase 2: The Move-In Experience

Moving into Mikontalo is high-energy. We focus on protecting your furniture in the tight corridors and ensuring nothing is damaged in transit.

Our In-House Services include:

Furniture Assembly

Need help with that new IKEA desk? We handle assembly and placement so you can focus on unpacking your personal belongings.

Heavy Lifting

We handle sofas, mattresses, and appliances through Mikontalo's elevators, preventing wall scuffs and corner damage.

Phase 3: The TOAS Move-Out "Deposit-Safe" Cleaning

This is where we save you money. Failing a TOAS inspection can cost you hundreds of euros. Our cleaners act as your "last line of defense."

The "Inspection-Ready" Cleaning Scope:

  • The "Hidden" Areas: We pull the stove and fridge forward to clean the floor, wall, and sides—the #1 reason for failed inspections.
  • Wet Rooms: Deep scrubbing of the floor drain (lattiakaivo), shower tiles, and scale removal from faucets.
  • Ventilation: Cleaning of all air supply and exhaust vents to ensure they are free of dust and lint.
  • Window & Frame Detail: Cleaning of window glass and the tracks where dust and dead insects accumulate.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How much does it cost?

We charge a transparent hourly rate for moving, and a fixed, flat-rate quote for the move-out cleaning. This ensures you know your exact total cost before we begin.

Do you work weekends?

Yes. We understand that student schedules are demanding. We operate 7 days a week, including early mornings and late evenings.

Can you handle international student moves?

Absolutely. We are familiar with the requirements for exchange students and can coordinate key handovers with the TOAS office or the R-Kioski pickup points.
